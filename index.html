About Us

✻

We have been in business for over 40 years and are a staple of the local community!

Paul Horan

Chef Proprietor

Chef Paul started his chef's training at Rockwell College at the age of 16. In the 70's he moved to England to continue his training.

On his return to Ireland he designed and established the Stone Court restaurant in Waterford, in partnership with his good friend, Frank Hamm.

Some years later he opened the Esquire in Tramore, where he has become renowned for his fine cuisine.

A little bit about the Pub

“Top Nosh” Liz Ryan, food critic

The Esquire was opened in 1932 by local business man Jim Quinn as a Gentleman's Bar. Taken over by Chef Paul Horan in 1980 and soon after opened one of Waterford first good food restaurants.

All food is served fresh and plenty of local seafood specials are available seasonally. All meat dishes are sourced from leading suppliers of the highest quality.

The Pub itself is a mix of traditional Irish and modern chic and has plenty of interesting items to keep you entertained including a bicycle on the ceiling!
We have hundreds of Whiskeys, Brandys, Gins and other unusual drinks on display.
